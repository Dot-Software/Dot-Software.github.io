Say Hello To The Pinboard

What better way to control your computer than at the press of a button?
With the pinboard, all the features of Winhancements are always accessible, whatever application you're in.







The pinboard is a culmination of everything new; a place where you can save colors, make notes and even surf the web.







And all you need to do to access the pinboard is press F2

Declutter Your Desktop

Your desktop is the first thing you see when you login, so why should it be anything less than a work of art?
The ability to hide your desktop icons turns it into a picturesque canvas ready to be painted on.

Built In Color Picker

Enhance Your Design

Colors are everywhere, especially in design. The ability to get the color code for anything on screen
